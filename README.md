## AI Interaction Overview
- Tools used: ChatGPT5, ChatGPT-4o
- Roles in the build:
  - Messaging strategy to reframe ScentSafe as a Social Wellness technology.
  - Content drafting for hero, mission, problem/solution, vision, FAQ, and testimonials.
  - Information architecture to organize awards, team, process, and CTAs.
  - Microcopy and tone alignment for safety, empathy, and impact.
  - Conversion-focused CTA and section labeling tailored to drivers, families, and B2B partners.

## Prompting Details
Below are 5 specific prompts I used to creatively leverage AI during the website build, focusing on pivoting ScentSafe toward Social Wellness:

1) Prompt: “Reposition ScentSafe from a hardware gadget to a Social Wellness technology. Propose a one-line value proposition, a subhead, and three proof-driven bullets that emphasize preventing drowsy driving as a public health outcome.”
   - Response summary: Generated the whole mainframe on how people should see ScentSafe as a social innovation or a bridge that safeguard the safety of drivers and the society. Highlighting that Transportation industry is the crucial bonding to connect everyone in the society.

2) Prompt: “Craft a mission narrative in 120–160 words that frames ‘Transportation Wellness’ as community care. Use inclusive language (drivers, families, operators, city planners) and a calm, trustworthy tone.”
   - Response summary: Produced a cohesive mission paragraph (“Safeguarding the backbone of our society”) that tied drivers’ well-being to safer roads and resilient cities. I adapted this into the Mission and Vision blocks.

3) Prompt: “Translate technical features into human outcomes. Map: ‘dynamic scent rotation, cold-receptor activation, real-time driver signals’ → benefits for drivers, fleet managers, and the public. Output a 3-step ‘How it works’ with a life-saving emphasis.”
   - Response summary: Delivered a clear 3-step model (Sense → Stimulate → Sustain) with benefits per stakeholder. I used this to label the ‘How does it Work?’ section and to simplify the FAQ explanation.

4) Prompt: “Write conversion-focused CTAs for three audiences: (a) individual drivers, (b) families of drivers, (c) fleet operators. Keep each to 4–6 words, safety-first tone.”
   - Response summary: Provided options like “Stay Alert, Get Home Safe” (drivers), “Keep Your Loved Ones Safe” (families), and “Protect Every Shift Today” (fleets). I placed these CTAs across hero, testimonials, and footer.

5) Prompt: “Turn raw awards and testimonials into social proof that supports Social Wellness. Create a compact awards ribbon and pull-quotes that highlight community impact rather than product hype.”
   - Response summary: Suggested an awards strip with dates and impact tags, and edited testimonials to foreground safer shifts and family confidence. I used these to structure the Awards and Testimonials sections.

## Project Evolution
- Goal shift: From “cool scent tech” to “Social Wellness infrastructure” that reduces drowsy-driving risk and protects communities.
- Content iterations: Multiple AI-assisted passes to remove duplication, unify tone, and emphasize outcomes (lives saved, safer shifts, family confidence).
- Structure: Clarified sections—Problem → Solution → Vision → How It Works → Proof (Awards, Testimonials) → Team → FAQ → CTAs—so the story flows from urgency to trust to action.
- Result: A site that communicates ScentSafe as a public-good technology with practical adoption paths for individuals and B2B partners.
